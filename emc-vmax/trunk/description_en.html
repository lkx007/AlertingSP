Summary:

Report Input/Output Operations Per Second (IOPS) on Disks, directors and devices (LUN) to measure performance and assure service availability. Alert consolidation from EMC VMAX and shown on All Alerts Console.

Main reports:

  • Capacity Usage
  • Pools Capacity Detailed pool capacity report
  • IOPS on Disks, directors and devices (LUN)
  • Throughput (MB/s) on Disks, directors and devices (LUN)
  • Array summary Detailed per array capacity and performance reports
  • All Alerts Console

Support list:

  • Symmetrix DMX-4 series
  • Symmetrix DMX-3 series
  • Symmetrix DMX-4 950 series
  • Symmetrix VMAX series
  • Symmetrix VMAX SE series

Requirements:

  • Solutions Enabler 7.6.1.0 & +
  • Unisphere for VMAX 1.6.1.8 & +
