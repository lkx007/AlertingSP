Summary:

Easily understand Brocade FC Switch performance and availability metrics with the SolutionPack for Brocade FC Switch. Access performance data information that was automatically collected and interpreted (using resource grouping and mathematical calculations) from across multiple fabrics.

Main reports:

  • Port Utilization: Report over Fibre channel ports.
  • Frames per second inbound, outbound, and discarded.
  • Throughput, inbound and outbound.
  • Port status errors.
  • Fiber Channel fabric inventory.

Support list:

  • Fabric OS > V4.x
